Project Overview

A Private Retreat
Above the Clouds

Hazelwood is a private residential community nestled in the verdant green hills of Chail, Himachal Pradesh. Spread across 3.5 acres of pristine mountain land, the project features 162 thoughtfully designed studio and 1BHK apartments across five elegant towers.

Conceived for peaceful mountain living, Hazelwood marries contemporary architecture with the raw, unhurried beauty of the Himalayan landscape — creating a home that is both an investment and an experience.

Why Chail

Where Nature
Meets Opportunity

Chail is not just a hill station — it is one of Himachal's hidden gems, rising in value, tourism, and lifestyle appeal with every passing year.

🌡️

Cool Climate Year Round

Chail sits at 2,250m above sea level with pleasant temperatures throughout the year — perfect for living and vacationing.

🌬️

Clean Himalayan Air

Pristine air quality, ancient deodar forests, and zero industrial pollution make Chail a sanctuary for health-conscious residents.

🏞️

Thriving Tourist Destination

Home to the world's highest cricket ground and the historic Chail Palace, tourism here grows year-on-year.

🏡

Weekend Home Potential
